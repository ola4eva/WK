Payslips are legal proof of the salary that a person draws from the employer. It is very important for every employee as it is a documental proof that the person is working in a particular company at the mentioned salary. If you want your employees to work efficiently then their satisfaction becomes really important for you.

Hence, offering employees payslip timely is a crucial part as it is an essential for them. However, it's not so easy to offer the payslips to employees manually. Being an established company, you need to look for an efficient way to provide payslips to your employees.

If you are running Odoo to manage your business, then most of your business related problems are solved. Now, you can easily share the payslips of your employees in Odoo. The module Odoo Employee Payslip Send By Email allows you to send the payslips to the respective employees via email.

Share the respective Payslips to the employees in Bulk

Now, you can easily reduce your laborious efforts and save your time in Odoo. The module Odoo Employee Payslip send by email allows you to send the respective payslips to multiple employees at a time in Odoo.

The module facilitates you to share the payslips to the employees via mail so that employees do not face any type of issue in getting the payslip.

The payslips are the confidential part of an employee; hence, it is made sure that an employee receives his/her payslips only. The module helps you to successfully save your time as well as efforts.

Let the Customers Get their Payslip of the desired time-period in Odoo

The module facilitates your employees to get the payslips of the desired time period in their email. The employee can only get his/her payslip so that the privacy of other employees is not hampered in any way.

It saves the time of employees, as with the help of the module, the employees can get their payslips easily. Otherwise, they would have to ask the Human Resource Executives manually. This process could be time taking as well as contains efforts of multiple resources.

Highlighted Features

Below is the detailed list of features of Odoo Employee Payslip Send By Email.
  • Share the Payslips to employees via mail
  • You can send the payslips to the employee whom the payslip belongs only
  • You can share the payslips to employees in bulk.
  • The employees can share only their payslips.
  • The employees can set the time period for which they want to get the payslip.
